2 Executive Summary

FishFinderMounts.com is a niche e-commerce manufacturer with a genuine product story and real engineering expertise — but the website severely under-leverages these strengths. The site suffers from five systemic problems:

Performance Crisis
Mobile LCP is 15.2s (6x over "poor" threshold) due to no page caching, a 7MB video, and 528KB HTML bloat.
Content Desert
Zero blog posts, zero buying guides, thin category pages with no text, missing meta descriptions site-wide.
AI Invisibility
GPTBot and Google-Extended blocked in robots.txt. Almost no citable prose for AI to extract.
Security Gap
Zero security headers on an e-commerce site processing payments. PHP version exposed.
Conversion Dead-Ends
No CTA visible above the fold on mobile. The most prominent CTA ("Continue Shopping") links to /# — a dead link. Product Wizard buried at y=1452.
