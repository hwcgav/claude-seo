# Platform-Specific AI Optimization Reference (March 2026)

Only 11% of domains are cited by both ChatGPT AND Google AI Overviews for the same query — platform-specific optimization is essential.

## Google AI Overviews

**Reach:** 48%+ of queries (Feb 2026), up 58% YoY. 1.5B users/month across 200+ countries.

**CTR Impact:** Organic CTR dropped 61% when AI Overviews appear, but cited brands earn 35% more organic CTR than non-cited competitors.

**Ranking Factors (by importance):**

1. **Semantic completeness** is the #1 factor. Pages scoring 8.5/10+ on topical coverage are 4.2x more likely to be cited.
2. **Multimodal content** (text + images + video + schema) significantly boosts selection. Content with multi-modal elements sees 156% higher selection rates.
3. **Authoritative citations** = +132% visibility. Citing primary sources and being cited by authoritative sites both matter.
4. **Top-10 ranking** still matters but is declining as a prerequisite (was 76% in mid-2025, now dropping). 92% of citations still come from top-10, but 47% come from below position 5 — demonstrating different selection logic from traditional organic.
5. **Schema triple stack**: Article + ItemList + FAQPage combination = 1.8x more citations vs Article schema alone.

**Optimization checklist:**
- Ensure comprehensive topical coverage (answer the query fully, don't leave sub-questions unanswered)
- Include relevant images, video embeds, and structured data
- Cite authoritative primary sources
- Implement Article + ItemList + FAQPage schema where applicable
- Maintain traditional SEO fundamentals (still needed for initial ranking)

## ChatGPT

**Reach:** 900M weekly active users.

**Ranking Factors (by importance):**

1. **Referring domains** are the #1 predictor. Sites with 350K+ referring domains average 8.4 citations per session.
2. **Content position matters:** 44.2% of citations come from the first 30% of page content. Front-load key information.
3. **Content freshness is critical:** Content updated within 30 days gets 3.2x more citations vs content >90 days old.
4. **Wikipedia is the default knowledge layer** — referenced in approximately 1 in 6 conversations.
5. **Retrieval != citation:** 85% of retrieved pages are never actually cited. Being crawled is necessary but not sufficient.
6. **Turn depth effect:** Turn 1 queries are 2.5x more likely to trigger citations than turn 10 in a conversation.

**Key insight:** Earned media and third-party coverage significantly outperform first-party content for ChatGPT citations. 91% of AI answers cite sites other than the brand's own domain.

**Optimization checklist:**
- Build referring domain profile through PR, guest content, and industry coverage
- Front-load critical information in the first 30% of content
- Keep content fresh (update within 30-day windows)
- Ensure Wikipedia presence for brand entity
- Optimize for direct, extractable answers (not just traffic-driving content)

## Perplexity

**Reach:** 500M+ monthly queries.

**Key Characteristics:**

1. **Reddit dominance:** 24% of Perplexity citations came from Reddit in Jan 2026 (6.6% overall across AI platforms, growing 73% YoY).
2. **YouTube** recently overtook Reddit as the #1 social source broadly for AI citations.
3. **Continuous web crawling:** Unlike ChatGPT (which uses Bing index), Perplexity crawls the web continuously, meaning fresh content gets indexed faster.
4. **Community validation:** Content that has been upvoted, discussed, and validated by communities gets preferential treatment.
5. **Recency rewarded:** Fresh, recently-published content ranks higher than older authoritative content in many cases.

**Optimization checklist:**
- Maintain active Reddit presence in relevant subreddits (genuine participation, not spam)
- Create YouTube content with keyword-rich titles, descriptions, and transcripts
- Publish frequently to take advantage of continuous crawling
- Earn community engagement (upvotes, replies, shares)
- Ensure PerplexityBot is allowed in robots.txt

## Bing Copilot

**Key Characteristics:**
- Sources from Bing index — Bing SEO fundamentals apply
- IndexNow protocol support for faster indexing
- Authoritative site preference

**Optimization checklist:**
- Submit URLs via IndexNow for rapid Bing indexing
- Ensure Bing Webmaster Tools is configured
- Follow Bing-specific SEO best practices (may differ from Google)
